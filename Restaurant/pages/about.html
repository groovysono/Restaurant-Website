Our Story

   Polly'splates was created in 1985 by Chef Polly as a small diner created for a small community in the heart of Dallas Texas. Polly drew in the people of Dallas with his classic plates such as Polly's Pasta, Polly's Soup, and of course Polly's Pancakes. Throughout the years, Polly'splates has grown into an upscale diner with a variety of American Classics.

   With two locations in Dallas and Fort Worth, Polly'splates is looking to expand to various locations across the USA. We pride ourselves on bringing a bit of home to our guests and giving them an unforgettable dining experience.
